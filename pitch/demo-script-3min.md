# 3-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20 Problem
- "This project solves education continuity for low-connectivity and disrupted learners in ASEAN."
- "Learners lose progress when internet is unstable, and facilitators cannot build localized courses fast enough."

## 0:20-0:55 Beneficiary Onboarding
- Show onboarding modal.
- Set segment (youth/educator/displaced/community org), connectivity level, language, and goal.
- Explain that this context adapts AI output and low-bandwidth delivery.

## 0:55-1:40 AI Course Generation
- Enter a topic and generate.
- Show module structure and one generated lesson step.
- Highlight adaptive behavior: low-bandwidth mode avoids heavy video dependency and emphasizes text-first activities.

## 1:40-2:10 Offline Continuity
- Click "Download course".
- Switch to offline mode (or describe), then open the downloaded course from account list.
- Continue learning while AI generation/edit is disabled offline.

## 2:10-2:40 Impact + Community
- Show KPI cards (skill gain, confidence gain, completion).
- Publish course as public/private.
- Show cohort create/join action.
- Explain moderation and reporting for responsible sharing.

## 2:40-3:00 Close
- "This is aligned to SDG 4, 8, and 10, with measurable impact and ASEAN-scalable design."
- "The solution is practical for youth, educators, and community organizations in low-resource settings."
